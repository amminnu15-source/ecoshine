🌱 OUR STORY

Born from a Better Idea

In 2016, founder Maria Chen read the ingredient labels on her cleaning products after having her first child. Formaldehyde. Ammonia. Synthetic fragrance chemicals. She knew there had to be a better way.

She spent a year researching and testing plant-based alternatives — and discovered they worked just as well, often better. EcoShine was born with a mission to make eco-friendly cleaning accessible and affordable for every family.

Today, with a team of 30+ certified eco-cleaners and 10,000+ homes cleaned, we're proud to be Portland's most trusted green cleaning service.
